Dear All;

As you prepare to make your way to job, we’ve created the Daily COVID-19 Health Check this web site, a quick and convenient way for you to monitor your health during your working place period.


Daily Health Check

This daily health check is required for all staff preparing to travel to job. It is an essential part of how we work together to keep you and other members of our community safe.


First day

All staff working in the workplace are required to start using the website from the first day, and continue to use it during their workplace travels and throughout the week.



14 Days Quarantine Time

Employees living outside the workplace must use the website every day during their 14-day on-site stay. If you begin to experience symptoms of COVID-19 during this period, please follow the directions on the website and contact your company's healthcare service.
